## Production Notes

### Video Specs
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Frame Rate**: 60fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9

### Screen Recording Settings
- **Screen Resolution**: Scaled to show clear text (1440p recommended)
- **Cursor**: Visible with yellow highlight
- **Keystroke visualization**: Show ‚åò+Shift+Space press

### Voiceover
- **Voice**: Professional, energetic male or female
- **Pace**: Fast but clear (120-140 WPM)
- **Tone**: Exciting, confident, helpful
- **Recording**: Studio quality, no background noise

### Music
- **Style**: Upbeat tech/startup music
- **Volume**: 20-30% (don't overpower voiceover)
- **Track example**: "Inspiring Technology" or "Modern Achievement"

### Graphics
- **Font**: SF Pro Display (Apple's system font)
- **Colors**:
  - Primary: #007AFF (Apple blue)
  - Success: #34C759 (green)
  - Background: #FFFFFF (white)
- **Animations**: Smooth, quick (0.3s transitions)

### On-Screen Text
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds each
- **Animation**: Fade in from left, fade out
- **Size**: Large enough to read on mobile (48px minimum)
